## Ingredients
* Bread flour
* Starter
* Salt
* Water
* Can of Olives
* Dried Thyme (eyeball about as much as salt in space.  Maaaybe 1tbsp or so)

## Method
* Follow [basic sourdough](/recipe/white-sourdough-bread) recipe.
* After autolyse step, mix in the olives and thyme
* Rest as normal
